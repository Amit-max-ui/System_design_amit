Agentic RAG on Kubeflow. 

Q. What is Agentic RAG?
It introduces autonomous agents capable of dynamic decision-making and workflow optimization. Unlike static systems, Agentic RAG employs iterative refinement and adaptive retrieval strategies to address complex, real-time, and multi-domain queries. This paradigm leverages the modularity of retrieval and generation processes while introducing agent-based autonomy.

Use case of Agentic RAG: 
- Customer support
- Financial analytics
- Adaptive learning platforms, 
where dynamic adaptability and contextual precision are paramount.


Concepts:
- RabbitAI integration for automatic testing for PR. 

Terms: 
- Orchestration mechanism. 
- Contextual Integration.
